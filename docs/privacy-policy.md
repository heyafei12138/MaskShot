# MaskShot Privacy Policy

MaskShot processes screenshots and selected images on device.

- The app does not require an account.
- The app does not upload screenshots or selected images.
- The app does not store image history.
- Photo library access is used only to find the latest screenshot when the user chooses that flow.
- Exported images are newly rendered bitmaps intended to avoid carrying source image metadata.

Contact: support@maskshot.app
